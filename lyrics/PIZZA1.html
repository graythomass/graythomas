Something Old and Nameless

We must be back to back, you and me
With something old and nameless
Stuck right between
And I’m blameless
My tongue is chained
To something you don’t perceive
And it took me
Said go back home now
You don’t live here anymore
Now you’re nameless
Bright eyes darken and sink into your skull
Like the lies I built for you
Call them forces outside of my control
That’s bullshit
We were on the path, you and me
Couldn’t sniff out what came between
So I dug through
Disgusting memories
Until I found what ate at me
I was silenced
Green eyes redden, no fanfare for the queen
Just hateful melodies
Though they’re beautiful, undeniably
They’re bullshit

Written by Graham Thomas McClure
