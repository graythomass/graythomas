. . . . . . . . Background . . . . . . . . 


Gray Thomas is a solo artist based in Minneapolis, Minnesota.
He released his first single, Europa, in January 2020.
His most recent release is titled Buonanotte, a 9
track album.  Work for the record began in Sept. 2021,
with it finally being released on June 20th, 2025.


Mainly inspired by the songwriting of such names
as Conor Oberst, Sam Beam, and Jim Croce, he sets
long-winded and meandering lyrics to smooth,
lush arrangements with a modern indie style.

. . . . . . . Other Projects . . . . . . . 



Boredshot (formerly The Look Was The S) was a shoegazey post-grunge
band which formed in 2018.  Gray joined the band under its former name
back in 2021 as a guitarist and backup vocalist.  The band broke up
in September 2024, immediately after their last show at Thesis Beer Project
in Rochester, MN.


He joined Neverwreck in July of 2023, a few years after
the band was formed in December 2021.  Neverwreck was 
a grunge / psychedelic rock band from Rochester, MN.
He was a member until the breakup in May 2024.  
The group's final gig was at Midwest Music Fest '24 in Winona, MN.


More recently, he has been part of Minneapolis based
acoustic trio Callisto with contemporaries Henry (Neverwreck)
and Kennedy (Sornione).  The three members often collaborate
on each other's music.
